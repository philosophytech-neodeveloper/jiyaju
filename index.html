About Us

Jiyaju-Eco is a health-centred, people-focused sanitizing/disinfecting service, delivering a turnkey business solution that is innovative, inclusive, and engaging. In a world
where hygiene is often neglected, our mission is to eradicate the spread of germs and diseases and to avoid bacteria from penetrating from one person to the next. We have
dedicated ourselves to educating and start the conversation of health around public domain surfaces such as Automatic Teller Machines (ATM’s); bathroom entrance/exits;
mall entrances/exits; airports; elevator buttons; escalators; hospital entrances/exits; digital pay point machines and various biometric mechanisms. We plan to place our
Jiyaju-Eco sanitizers/disinfectant machines in and around these areas to mitigate infection and promote hygiene.

Such areas where high volumes of people are in contact with each other, pose a great threat to human lives as they are possible hosts to microbial organisms and germs
that cause sickness and infection and can possibly start a pandemic like the Coronavirus if proper hygiene and sanitation measures are not followed.

Our Service Units
We specialise in the provision of specialised hygiene solutions while improving sanitisation/disinfection and quality of life. Our hand sanitizers are 99.9% effective against
most common germs. Our service is carried out by our specialised Jiyaju-Eco workforce that make sure our machines are always presentable and ready to serve the greater
public.

It’s as easy as reaching out your hand into our cylindrical illuminated hand dispensing area and the unit dispenses either sanitizers or disinfectant onto the palm of your
hand. You are also presented with relevant health and safety content while sanitizing/disinfecting your hands. Each machine is clearly labelled whether it is a SANITIZER or
a DISIFECTANT, this is for the safety of children and people who are allergic or react to alcohol based solitions.
